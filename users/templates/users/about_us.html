ABOUT US

Bookspot is an online bookstore with a mission to support users exchange and find their books with ease.
We believe that bookstores are essential to a healthy culture. We believes in the reincarnation of the books. We believe in making them immortal and make them live forever. The books which were going to be wasted in someone's house have been reused and given a new life. The life span of books is increased when we provide them to our Readers again. We save trees by saving books from getting wasted

Thanks for being part of our community.
